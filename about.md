# About Me

My name is Toka (which is also the [Albanian name of the Earth](https://sq.wikipedia.org/wiki/Toka)) Alokda (which is also the Arabic name of [Alpha Piscium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alpha_Piscium)), and I come from a beautiful city in the north of Egypt called [Alexandria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexandria). I did my Bachelor's in Physics with a concentration in Astrophysics at the [University of Science and Technology at Zewail City](https://www.zewailcity.edu.eg/) in [Giza](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giza), and during that time I was living in [Cairo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cairo). In October 2021 I moved to [Bonn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bonn) to do my Master's in Astrophysics at the [University of Bonn](https://www.uni-bonn.de/en/), and ever since it has been a big adventure.

When I am not doing science I am also a singer, and I like to write, paint, learn to play musical instruments, and go up in some hills or mountains to hike. :)
