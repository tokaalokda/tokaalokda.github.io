# About Me

I come from a beautiful city in the north of Egypt called [Alexandria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexandria). I did my Bachelor's in Physics with a concentration in Astrophysics at the [University of Science and Technology at Zewail City](https://www.zewailcity.edu.eg/) in [Giza](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giza), and during this time I was living in [Cairo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cairo). I moved to [Bonn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bonn) for my Master's at the [University of Bonn](https://www.uni-bonn.de/en/) in October 2021, and ever since it has been a big adventure.

When I am not doing science or coding, I am also a singer. I also like to write, learn to play musical instruments, and often go up in some hills or mountains to hike. :)
